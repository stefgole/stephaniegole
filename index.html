About Me.

My name is Stephanie Gole, a freelance video editor based in Melbourne, Australia. Since 2016, I've been dedicated to bringing stories to life through the magic of video editing.

While I have experience in creating captivating property videos and commercial content, my recent focus has been on editing engaging social media videos for platforms like YouTube and Instagram. I'm all about crafting content that grabs attention and leaves a lasting impression.

Beyond my editing work, I have a deep passion for karate. In fact, my recent projects have involved creating vlog videos for an elite karate athlete, blending my love for the sport with my expertise in video editing.

One of the tools I love to use is Adobe After Effects, which allows me to take your videos to the next level with custom motion graphics. Whether it's adding stylish text animations, enhancing transitions, or incorporating dynamic elements, After Effects gives me the flexibility to bring your vision to life in unique and engaging ways.

With a Diploma of Screen and years of hands-on experience, I have the skills to turn your ideas into compelling visual stories. Whether you have a clear vision or need help bringing your ideas to life, I'm here to collaborate with you every step of the way.

You can check out some of my past work in the portfolio section. Let's team up and create something extraordinary together!
